General FAQs

How long does it take to build a website?
The timeline depends on the project scope. A basic site may take 2–4 weeks, while a complex custom website could take 6–12 weeks.
Do you offer custom website designs or use templates?
We offer both. Most of our clients prefer custom designs tailored to their brand, but we can also work with templates for faster delivery.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
Yes, all websites we build are fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices.
Can I update the website myself after it's built?
Absolutely. We build sites with user-friendly CMS platforms like WordPress, HubSpot, or Webflow, so you can manage content easily.
Do you provide website hosting and domain registration?
We can guide you through hosting and domain setup, or handle it fully based on your preferences.
